# Design Brief

## Style: Creative Catalyst

The design should resemble an artistic studio, with a mix of traditional and digital elements. The atmosphere should be lively and encouraging, with plenty of whitespace to let the artwork shine. The UI should be intuitive and easy to navigate, with bold typography and vibrant colors. The overall picture should evoke a sense of creativity and community.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Vibrant and playful, with a focus on blues and oranges to represent art and inspiration |
| Typography | Bold and expressive, with a mix of serif and sans-serif fonts to add depth and visual interest |
| Components | Buttons and cards should have a handmade, artistic feel, with subtle texture and shading to give them a tactile quality |
| Imagery | User-generated artwork should be the focus, with plenty of showcases and galleries to highlight the community's creativity |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** blue
- **Mode:** light
- **Border Radius:** 0.5

## App Variant: ArtMentor Pro

> Unlock Artistic Potential

ArtMentor Pro focuses on professional artists and art schools, offering advanced AI-powered mentorship, portfolio management, and networking opportunities with galleries and collectors. The app provides in-depth analysis of artwork, suggestions for improvement, and access to exclusive art events. By targeting a more premium audience, ArtMentor Pro can offer higher-tier services and generate revenue through subscription-based models.

**Target Audience:** Professional artists and art schools
**Monetization:** Subscription-based model with tiered pricing
**Complexity:** premium
